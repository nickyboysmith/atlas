This Feature allows you to change your password.
We recommend that you use a “Strong” password.
A Strong Password would contain Alphabetic, in Upper and Lower case, numeric characters, a symbol and be at least 8 characters long.
“Sch3m3!0n3” (Scheme!one) is an example of a Strong Password which is easy to remember.
Notice how we replace the “e” with “3” and “o” with “0” (zero).
